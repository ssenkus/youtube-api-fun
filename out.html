Vid-Living in a Van Tip #42 - Craigslist Rideshare

there's a whole new generation of ambitious kids - the voice of America inspired by Craigslist Joe (or so it would seem). This is good news for us 'cause those Craigslist Joe kids will rideshare in your vehicle and pay your gas money! And this is a huge help when you're living in a van. Think of it as renting out your backroom to the crazy hippie you met on the street.

Vid-East Cack Presents: Trudy & Sharmini play saw duets

Thanks to Frentz & Songs for the generous use of their saws & space and for accomodating our needs even though it's a pretty weird request to play two saws at a hardware store. Trudy on tenor saw & Sharmini on soprano saw. Neither of us had any resin and we still rocked those metal cutting machines. Domination!
